About Brit-Buy

Our Story: Why We Started Brit-Buy

Like many online shoppers, we – brothers Steven and Jon Richardson – grew tired of the endless waiting for orders shipped from overseas. Finding a great deal was exciting, but the weeks-long delivery times often took the shine off.

We thought, "There has to be a better way!" So, we founded Brit-Buy, operating from our base here in West London, England.

Our goal is simple: offer you the low prices you love, but ship your items directly from the UK. We stock the kinds of popular gadgets, accessories, and everyday items you might usually buy from China, meaning you get them much faster – often in just a few days!

Our Core Values

  • On-Time Shipping: Getting your order to you reliably and promptly. We offer various UK shipping options, including Next Day, 1st Class, and 2nd Class, so you can choose the speed that suits you best.
  • Excellent Customer Handling: Providing friendly, helpful support to ensure a smooth experience.
  • Customer Happiness: Ultimately, our goal is to keep you happy with great products, fair prices, and efficient service.
  • Making sure you're happy: Ensuring you are happy with your purchase and the speed of delivery. Which is why we offer a choice of UK shipping speeds (including Next Day, 1st Class, and 2nd Class) so you can get your order when you need it.
